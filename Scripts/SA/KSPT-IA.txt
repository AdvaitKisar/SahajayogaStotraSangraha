Āvāhanaṃ Na Jānāmi Na Jānāmi Tavārcanam । 
Pūjāṃ Caiva Na Jānāmi Kṣamyatāṃ Parameśvari ॥1॥

Mantrahīnaṃ Kriyāhīnaṃ Bhaktihīnaṃ Sureśvari । 
Yatpūjitaṃ Mayā Devi Paripūrṇaṃ Tadastu Me ॥2॥

Gataṃ Pāpaṃ Gataṃ Duḥkhaṃ Gataṃ Dāridryameva Ca । 
Āgataṃ Paramacaitanyaṃ Puṇyo'haṃ Tava Darśanāt ॥3॥

Anyathā Śaraṇaṃ Nāsti Tvameva Śaraṇaṃ Mama ।
Tasmāt Kāruṇyabhāvena Rakṣa Rakṣa Parameśvari ॥4॥ 

Aparādhasahastrāṇi Kriyante'harniśaṃ Mayā । 
Dāso'yamiti Māṃ Matvā Kṣamasva Parameśvari ॥5॥
