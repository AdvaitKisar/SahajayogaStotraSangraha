Namāmi Sadguruṃ Śāntaṃ Pratyakṣaṃ Śivarūpiṇam । 
Śirasā Yogapīṭhasthaṃ Muktikāmārthasiddhaye ॥1॥

Gururbrahmā Gururviṣṇurgururdevo Maheśvaraḥ । 
Guruḥ Sākṣāt Parabrahma, Tasmai Śrīgurave Namaḥ ॥2॥

Ajñānatimirāndhasya, Jñānāñjanaśalākayā । 
Cakṣurunmīlitaṃ Yena Tasmai Śrīgurave Namaḥ ॥3॥

Tvaṃ Pitā Tvaṃ Ca Me Mātā, Tvaṃ Bandhustvaṃ Ca Devatā ।
Saṃsārapratibodhārthaṃ, Tasmai Śrīgurave Namaḥ ॥4॥

Yasya Kāraṇarūpasya, Kāryarūpeṇa Bhāti Yat । 
Kāryakāraṇarūpāya, Tasmai Śrīgurave Namaḥ ॥5॥

Śrīmatparabrahma Guruṃ (Śrīmātājī) Smarāmi, 
Śrīmatparabrahma Guruṃ (Śrīmātājī) Vadāmi ।
Śrīmatparabrahma Guruṃ (Śrīmātājī) Namāmi, 
Śrīmatparabrahma Guruṃ (Śrīmātājī) Bhajāmi ॥6॥
