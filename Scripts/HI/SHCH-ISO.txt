Jay Hanumān Gyān Guṇ Sāgar ।  Jay Kapīś Tihum̐ Lōka Ujāgar ॥
Rāmadūta Atulit Baladhāmā । Añjani Putra Pavanasut Nāmā ॥
Mahāvīr Vikram Bajraṅgī । Kumati Nivār Sumati Kē Saṅgī ॥
Kañcan Baraṇ Virāj Subēśā । Kānan Kuṇḍal Kuñcit Kēśā ॥
Hāth Bajra Au Dhvajā Birājai । Kāndhē Mūñja Janēū Sājai ॥
Śaṅkar Suvan Kēśarī Nandan । Tēj Pratāp Mahā Jag Vandan ॥
Vidyāvān Guṇī Ati Cātur । Rām Kāj Karibē Kō Ātur ॥
Prabhu Caritra Sunivē Kō Rasiyā । Rāmalakhan Sītā Man Basiyā ॥ 
Sūkṣmarūpadhari Siyahiṁ Dikhāvā । Vikaṭ Rūpadhari Laṅk Jarāvā ॥
Bhīm Rūp Dhari Asur Saṅhārē । Rāmacandra Kē Kāj Saṁvārē ॥
Lāya Sañjīvan Lakhan Jiyāyē । Śrī Raghubīr Haraṣi Ur Lāyē ॥
Raghupati Kīnhīṁ Bahut Baṛāī । Tum Mama Priya Bharatahi Sam Bhāī 
Sahas Badan Tumharō Yaś Gāvaiṁ । As Kahi Śrīpati Kaṇṭh Lagāvaiṁ ॥
Sanakādik Brahmādi Munīśā । Nārad Śārad Sahit Ahīśā ॥
Yam Kubēr Dikpāl Jahāntē । Kavi Kōvid Kahi Sakaiṁ Kahāntē ॥
Tum Upakār Sugrīvahī Kīnhā । Rām Milāy Rājapad Dīnhā ॥
Tumharō Mantra Vibhīṣaṇ Mānā । Laṅkēśvar Bhay Sab Jag Jānā ॥
Yug Sahastra Yōjan Par Bhānū । Līlyō Tāhi Madhur Phal Jānū ॥
Prabhu Mudrikā Mēli Mukh Māhīṁ । Jaladhi Lāṅghi Gayē Acaraj Nāhīṁ ॥
Durgam Kāj Jagat Kē Jētē । Sugam Anugraha Tumharē Tētē ॥
Rām Duvārē Tum Rakhavārē । Hōt Na Āgyā Bin Paisārē ॥
Sab Sukh Lahaiṁ Tumhārī Sarnā । Tum Rakṣak Kāhū Kō Ḍarnā ॥
Āpan Tēj Samhārō Āpai । Tīnōṁ Lōk Hāṅkatē Kāmpai ॥
Bhūt Piśāc Nikaṭ Nahiṁ Āvaiṁ । Mahāvīr Jab Nām Sunāvaiṁ ॥
Nāśai Rōg Harai Sab Pīrā । Japat Nirantar Hanumat Bīrā ॥
Saṅkaṭ Sē Hanumān Chuṛāvē । Man Kram Bacan Dhyān Jō Lāvaiṁ ॥
Sab Par Rām Tapasvī Rājā । Tinkē Kāj Sakal Tum Sājā ॥
Aur Manōrath Jō Kōī Lāvai । Sōī Amit Jīvan Phal Pāvai ॥
Cahum̐ Ōr Pratāp Tumhārā । Hai Parasiddha Jagat Ujiyārā ॥
Sādhu Sant Kē Tum Rakhvārē । Asur Nikandan Rāma Dulārē ॥
Aṣṭasiddhi Nav Nidhi Kē Dātā । As Var Dīn Jānakī Mātā ॥
Rām Rasāyan Tumhārē Pāsā । Sadā Rahō Raghupati Kē Dāsā ॥
Tumharē Bhajan Rāma Kō Bhāvai । Janma Janma Kē Dukh Bisrāvai ॥
Ant Kāl Raghupati Pur Jāī । Jahām̐ Janam Haribhakt Kahāī ॥ 
Aur Dēvatā Citta Na Dharaī । Hanumat Sēī Sarva Sukh Karaī ॥ 
Saṅkaṭ Haṭai Miṭai Sab Pīrā । Jō Sumirai Hanumat Balabīrā ॥ 
Jai Jai Hanumān Gōsāī । Kr̥ipā Karahu Gurūdēv Kī Nāī ॥
Yah Śat Bār Pāṭh Kar Jōī । Chūṭahiṁ Bandi Mahāsukh Hōī ॥ 
Jō Yaha Paṛhē Hanumān Cālisā । Hōy Siddhī Sākhī Gaurīsā ॥ 
Tulsīdās Sadā Hari Cērā । Kījai Nāth Hr̥iday Mahaṁ Ḍērā ॥ 
Dōhā - Pavanatanay Saṅkaṭ Haran, Maṅgal Mūrti Rūp ॥
Rāmalakhan Sītā Sahit, Hr̥iday Basahu Surabhūp ॥
